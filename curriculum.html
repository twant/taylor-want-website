Curriculum Overview

Unit Topics Major Labs/Projects
Motion Vector and scalar quantities; Displacement, velocity, and acceleration in one and two dimensions; Kinematics equations; Free fall motion "Ticker Tape Timers"; "How fast can you...?"; "Free Falling Eggs"
Forces Properties and types of forces; Free-body force diagrams; Net force; Mass and Weight; Friction; Newton's Three Laws "Newton's Three Stations"; "Frictional Forces"; "Newton's Bottle Rockets"
Momentum Describe and calculate momentum; Total momentum of systems; Elastic, partially inelastic, and completely inelastic collisions; Separations "What is Momentum?"; "Impulse: The Egg and the Bedsheet"; "Inelastically Colliding Projectiles"
Energy Types of energy and energy conversion; Potential energy; Kinetic energy; Conservation of energy PHeT Skatepark Virtual Lab; "Impulse: The Egg and the Bedsheet"; "Inelastically Colliding Projectiles"
Heat Methods of heat transfer; Average molecular kinetic energy; Phase changes; Temperature changes Specific Heat Capacity of Milk; Solar House; Heat Stations
Electricity Basic circuitry; Circuit Analysis; Series Circuits; Parallel Circuits; Combination Circuits House Wiring; "Can you light a lightbulb?"
