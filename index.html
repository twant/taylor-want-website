About

Education

Ms. Want began her education at an Expeditionary Learning Outward Bound charter school in Denver, CO called Odyssey School of Denver, where she attended elementary and middle school. She went on to graduate from Denver's own East High School. East's rigorous approach prepared her well to do her undergraduate work at Carleton College, where she deepened her love of physics, computer science, and learning. She graduated in 2013 with a Bachelor of Arts in Physics degree. She went on to receive her Master of Education degree from Boston University in 2015

Teaching Career

Ms. Want began her teaching career as a 7th grade math teacher at Breakthrough Denver--Manual as a teaching intern in the summer of 2009. She went on to serve as a teaching intern at the Breakthrough Collaborative for three more summers, landing at Breakthrough San Francisco in 2010, Breakthrough Kent Denver in 2011, and Breakthrough Cambridge in 2012.

In 2013, Ms. Want joined Teach for America, which afforded her the great privilege of teaching physics at Durfee High School.
